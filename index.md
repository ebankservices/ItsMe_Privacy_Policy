# Privacy Policy for ItsMe app

Last updated: [11/1/2025]

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your personal information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect
- We don't collect personal information.
- Signatures that you sign are stored on your mobile phone.
- When you use "Send" button your signature will be stored on remote server under an account number you write.
- No other information are assoicated with your signatures.

## How We Use Your Information
- This app is a demonstration of the features that can be programmed and all stored signatures will be deleted shortly ,no more than week after signing.
